## Lemma.1 $\mathcal{L} \succeq 0$

<u>**proof**</u>

> **Theorem.1** $\bm{x}^T\mathcal{L}\bm{x}\ge 0 \Rightarrow \mathcal{L} \succeq 0$

$$\begin{align}
f_i&=\mathcal{L}_{ij}x_i\\
&=tbd
\end{align}$$

$$\begin{align}
\\bm{x}^T\mathcal{L}\bm{x}&=\sum_{v_i} x_i f_i\\
&=\sum_{v_i} x_i\sum_{v_j\in \mathcal{N}(v_i)}(x_i-x_j)
\end{align}$$

$\Box$
